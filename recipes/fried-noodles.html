Fried Noodles

This is a fried noodles, a cuisine that famous in asian countries.

Fried noodles, a dish that everyone(probably) likes. It comes in different shape and tastes. People have changes and improvised this dish overtime based on their preferences and region environments.

Some regions in China, they cooked their noodles a little bit more spicy than usual because of cold weather there need them to warm up their bodies all the time. But some in other asian countries such as Malaysia, they cooked fried noodles with less spicy because they already in not-so-warm country. So lets get started.

Ingredients

  • Some noodles.
  • Some minced onions and garlic.
  • Some soy sauces.
  • Some stirred raw eggs.
  • Some oyster sauces.
  • Some salt, sugar and MSG.
  • A little bit warm water.

Steps

  1. Heat up the oil and toss the minced onions and garlics.
  2. After a while, toss the stirred raw eggs and stir it slowly.
  3. Next, pour down the noodles slowly and slow down the fire.
  4. Keep stirring and toss the soy sauces, the oyster sauces, the salt, the sugar and the MSG.
  5. Mix it well. Then, slowly add a little bit of warm water and keep stirring.
  6. After you confident that the noodles is already cooked, close the fire and prepare it on plate neatly.
  7. Tadaaa. Here's your fried noodles Sir!.
