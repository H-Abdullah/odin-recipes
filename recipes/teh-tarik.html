Teh Tarik

This is Teh Tarik, a famous tea in asian countries.

Usually asian people drank this tea early in the morning and usually accompanied with kueh-mueh such as pisang goreng, karipap etc as side dishes.

But doesn't mean you cant drink it in the evening. This tea is rich with milky-tastes and a little bit bitter following with refreshing tea leaves whenever you took a sip from it. So without futher a due, Lets get started!.

Ingredients

  • Some tea bag.
  • Some milk.
  • Some sugar.
  • A pinch of salt.
  • One or two cardamom

Steps

  1. Prepare a hot boiling water.
  2. Dip down the tea bag and stir it slowly for a quarter minute.
  3. Pour down the milk and continue to stir it slowly.
  4. Add the sugar, a pinch of salt and the cardamom.
  5. Stir it again but slowly.
  6. After a minute stirring, remove the cardamom and the tea bag.
  7. Tadaaa. Here you go, a cup of Teh Tarik that can warm up your day.
